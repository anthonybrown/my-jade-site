Node, Express, Jade, Stylus.

A skeleton made with my favorite node tools. Express handles the routing. Jade takes care of the boring HTML markup and replaces it with an awesome language that is... well liberating. Stylus, my favorite out of the CSS pre-processors.It gives me the most freedom out of the ones I have used.SaSS and LESS and a few others, I pick Stylus each time if I can. Absolutely love working with it!

Basically I made this page so I would have a reference.I am constantly exploring new tools and don't want to forget how the syntax.
